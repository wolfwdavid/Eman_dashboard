# DID Voice Intake — answers the bot uses to fill applications in Eman's words

Eman answers these once (type OR send voice notes — Whisper transcribes them). The drafter reads this
file so grant applications reflect DID's real story, numbers, and Eman's voice — not the model's guesses.
Short, honest answers are fine; more specifics = better drafts.

## 1. The story & mission
- In 1–2 sentences, what is Diversity Includes Disability and why did you start it?
- What's the core problem you're trying to change? (in your own words)
- One line you'd want every funder to remember about DID.

## 2. What you actually do (programs/services)
- List your main programs/services (trainings, consulting, representation modeling, speaking, …).
- For each: who it's for, and what changes because of it.
- A recent example or win you're proud of (a training, a client, a talk, a moment).

## 3. Proof & numbers (whatever you have)
- People reached / trained / served (rough numbers + timeframe are fine).
- Organizations or partners you've worked with.
- Any testimonials or quotes you're allowed to share.

## 4. The ask & the money
- If a funder asked "what would you do with the money?" — what's your honest answer?
- Typical amount you'd request, and roughly how you'd spend it (buckets are fine).
- Your annual budget ballpark (or "pre-budget / early-stage").

## 5. Eligibility facts (so the bot never guesses)
- Legal status today (S-corp LLC, 501(c)(3) pending — anything changed?).
- Do you have a fiscal sponsor yet? If so, who?
- City/region you serve.

## 6. Eman's voice
- Words/phrases you use a lot (or ones you never want in your applications).
- Tone: warm? direct? bold? plainspoken? (pick a couple)
- Anything you're NOT willing to say or overclaim.

## 7. Standard application answers (reuse across grants)
- **Need statement:** why this work matters now (3–4 sentences).
- **Goals:** what success looks like in the next year.
- **Why DID/Eman:** what makes you the right person/org to do this.
- **Evaluation:** how you'd know it worked.
